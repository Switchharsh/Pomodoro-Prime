    Welcome to Pomodoro Prime

    Your focus companion. Work in focused blocks, track distractions, and build a streak.

    🌳
    Pomodoro Timer 25 min focus + 5 min break cycles. Watch the sun and moon cross the sky as you work.
    📋
    Task Tracking Add tasks, pick one to focus on, and check them off as you go.
    🧠
    Distraction Log Quick-tap when distracted. Review patterns to find your focus triggers.
    🫁
    Breathing Guide 4-7-8 breathing exercise. Use anytime to calm your mind.

    Quick Start

    1

    Add tasks in the Tasks panel

    2

    Pick a task in the topic dropdown and click Start

    3

    Focus until the timer ends, then take a break

    Keyboard Shortcuts

    Space Start / Pause timer
    R Reset timer
    T Toggle dark mode
    S Skip to next mode
    F Toggle focus mode
    Esc Close modals / Exit focus mode
    D Log distraction (during focus)
